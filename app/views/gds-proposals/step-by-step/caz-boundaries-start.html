Check the Clean Air Zone boundaries

National overview

There are currently two Charging Clean Air Zones. These are Leeds and Birmingham.

Leeds

Leeds operates a CAZ B, meaning that it only charges non-compliant lorries, buses and Taxi/PHvs.

Leeds' boundary includes anything within the: A6120, A647, B6154, M621, A61, A63 and M1.
This can be visualised on Leeds' Clean Air Zone boundary map.

For more information about the Leeds' Clean Air Zone, visit their site.
