Can I claim a refund?

You cannot claim a refund:

  • if you didn't drive into a Clean Air Zone on a day which you've paid for
  • for any day that has already started

You can claim a refund:

  • if you have paid for a compliant vehicle
  • if you have paid for an exemept vehicle
  • if you had to pay because DVLA records were incorrect

If you are eligible for a refund click continue.
