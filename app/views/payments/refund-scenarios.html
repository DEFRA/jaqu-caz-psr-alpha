Can I claim a refund?

You can claim a refund:

  • if you paid in advance but now need to cancel one or more days (you'll need to apply for this refund by midnight before the first day you paid for)
  • if you have paid for a vehicle that meets clean air standards
  • if you have paid for a vehicle that is exempt (you must have registered your exempt vehicle with the local authority or you won't be able to claim a refund)
  • if you had to pay because the DVLA's records for your vehicle were incorrect

You can't claim a refund:

  • if you paid for a day but didn't drive in the zone on that day
  • if the day you want to claim a refund for has already started
