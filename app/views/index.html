Pay a Clean Air Zone charge

If your vehicle doesn't meet clean air standards, you need to pay a daily charge for driving it into a Clean Air Zone.

Use this service to pay a charge for driving in a Clean Air Zone.

You'll need the registration number of the vehicle.

Check if your vehicle meets clean air standards or discover how you can avoid having to pay.

You must pay the Clean Air charge by midnight the day after you drive through a Clean Air Zone.
